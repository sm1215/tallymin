Copy Results

Click into an editable field, like Score, and press Ctrl+C / Cmd+C to copy all names and scores to your clipboard. You don't need to highlight anything, just have a blinking cursor. The output will be wrapped in triple backticks which can help with formatting when pasting into most messenger / chat programs. You can paste the whole output into discord for example.
